# Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyses retail sales performance, profitability, discount impact, product performance and geographic trends using Power BI.

## Business Problem

Retail managers require visibility into sales drivers, profit performance and discount effectiveness to support better business decisions.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Executive Dashboard
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Orders
- Quantity
- Average Discount

### 2. Product Performance
- Sales by Sub-Category
- Profit by Sub-Category
- Discount vs Profit Analysis

### 3. Geographic Intelligence
- Sales by Region
- Profit by State
- Sales by City
- Geographic Mapping

### 4. Profitability & Discount Analysis
- Profit Margin Analysis
- Discount Impact Analysis
- Top and Bottom Performing Products

### 5. Business Insights & Recommendations
- Key Findings
- Business Impact
- Strategic Recommendations

## Key Findings

- Phones generated the highest sales.
- Copiers generated the highest profit.
- Higher discounts were associated with lower profitability.
- Sales and profitability varied across regions and categories.

## Recommendations

- Review discount strategies.
- Focus on high-margin products.
- Maintain stock availability for high-demand products.
- Investigate low-performing categories.
